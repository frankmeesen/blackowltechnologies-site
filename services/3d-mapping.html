What We Do

Data-Backed Decisions From Above

Our photogrammetric drone surveys capture thousands of overlapping high-resolution images in a single flight. These are processed using advanced photogrammetry software to produce centimetre-accurate 3D models, dense point clouds, orthophoto mosaics and Digital Elevation Models.

Whether you need to track earthwork volumes on a construction site, produce an accurate survey of a land parcel, or create a 3D model of a building or infrastructure asset — our mapping service delivers in a fraction of the time and cost of traditional survey methods.

RTK GNSS positioning is used throughout to ensure spatial accuracy without the need for ground control points on every mission.

Applications

Where 3D Mapping Adds Value

01
Construction Site Monitoring

Regular survey flights track earthwork progress, cut-and-fill volumes and site development over time. Compare as-built against design with precision — reducing rework and disputes.

02
Land Surveying

Produce accurate topographic surveys of undeveloped land, agricultural parcels and natural terrain. Suitable for planning applications, land registration and civil engineering design.

03
Volume Calculation

Measure stockpile volumes, quarry extraction and landfill levels with high accuracy. Ideal for quarrying, mining, waste management and logistics operations.

04
Asset & Progress Reporting

Deliver recurring aerial surveys at defined intervals to track project progress, asset condition and site changes over time — with each dataset aligned to the same coordinate system.

Process

From Flight to Final Data

01
Mission Planning

We design a flight plan tailored to your required coverage, accuracy and output format. Ground Control Points are placed where needed, and all airspace authorisations are arranged.

02
Aerial Data Capture

Our RTK-enabled mapping drone systematically covers the survey area, capturing overlapping imagery at precise altitude and overlap settings for maximum reconstruction quality.

03
Photogrammetric Processing

Captured images are processed using professional photogrammetry software to generate a dense point cloud, 3D mesh, orthophoto mosaic and Digital Elevation Model.

04
Data Delivery

All outputs are delivered in your preferred format (LAS, GeoTIFF, OBJ, DXF, PDF) via secure cloud link within 48 hours. Integration with your existing GIS or CAD software supported.
