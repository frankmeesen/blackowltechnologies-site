Multi-Sensor Survey

Combining thermal, RGB and LiDAR data in a single coordinated mission — for clients who need a complete picture of their site without scheduling multiple visits.

02
Research & Scientific Support

Supporting universities, environmental agencies and research institutes with aerial data collection, monitoring and documentation for scientific projects.

03
Event & Production Coverage

Coordinated aerial coverage for outdoor events, film productions, architectural reveals or corporate communications requiring precise timing and angles.

04
Emergency & Rapid Response

Fast-turnaround aerial assessment for incident response, damage evaluation or urgent site documentation where speed and accuracy are both critical.

Process

How We Build Your Mission

01
Brief & Discovery

You tell us what you need — the objective, site, timeline and any specific constraints. We ask the right questions to make sure we understand exactly what success looks like for you.

02
Mission Design

We design the mission plan, select the right equipment and sensors, and handle all regulatory authorisations required by Belgian and EU drone law.

03
Execution

Our certified pilot team executes the mission on site. We adapt in real time to conditions while maintaining strict safety and compliance standards throughout.

04
Delivery

You receive the data, imagery or report in the format you need — whether that's raw files for your own team, a processed dataset or a fully structured deliverable.
