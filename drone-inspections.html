What We Do

Eyes Where You Can't Send People

Traditional inspections of roofs, towers, bridges and industrial structures require scaffolding, rope access or crane hire — all of which cost time, money and introduce risk. Our drone inspection service eliminates all three.

We fly systematic, grid-based inspection patterns capturing ultra-high-definition imagery at every angle. Data is processed into annotated reports that flag defects, assign severity ratings and reference GPS-tagged locations — so your engineers know exactly where to act.

All flights are conducted by EASA-certified pilots in full compliance with Belgian aviation regulations. Our systems are fully insured for commercial operations.

Process

How a Drone Inspection Works

01
Scope & Site Assessment

We review your asset, agree on the inspection scope and identify any airspace restrictions or site access requirements. Flight authorisations are filed in advance.

02
On-Site Mission

Our pilot executes a systematic inspection flight, capturing high-resolution imagery at multiple angles and distances. Most missions are completed within a half-day.

03
Data Processing

Raw footage and images are reviewed and processed. Defects are identified, classified by severity and linked to GPS coordinates for exact location reference.

04
Report Delivery

A structured PDF report with annotated images, defect classifications and maintenance recommendations is delivered within 48 hours of the mission.
